THE PROBLEM

Attendance tracking shouldn't be a second job

Switching apps, forgetting to log in, manual HR entries — it adds up to wasted time and inaccurate data.

Before

Open browser → find Odoo → click Attendance → click Check In → go back to Slack

After

Type /login in Slack. Done. Odoo attendance updated instantly.

SLASH COMMANDS

Four commands. That's all your team needs.

/login

Records attendance in Odoo. Grace minutes applied automatically.

/logout

Closes the attendance record. Net working hours calculated instantly.

/break

Starts a timed break. Short breaks under threshold are not deducted.

/resume

Returns from break. Duration calculated and saved to Odoo.

Commands in action

/login → instant Odoo check-in

/break and /resume with duration

/logout with net working hours

SMART FEATURES

Thoughtful details that matter.

Login Grace Minutes

Type /login at 10:00 AM with a 5-minute grace window? Odoo records 9:55 AM. No more punishing employees for being 30 seconds late to type.

Minimum Break Threshold

Breaks under the configured minimum (e.g. 5 min) are saved but not deducted from working hours. A quick coffee doesn't penalise your team.

Net Working Hours

Net Hours = Total Hours − Counted Breaks — shown live in Odoo Attendance list and form view.

HMAC-SHA256 Verification

Every Slack request verified with your Signing Secret before any DB action. Replay attacks blocked with a 5-minute window.

Per-Employee Timezone

Times shown in each employee's own timezone — read from their HR record. Works globally.

Missed Logout Alerts

Employees who forgot to /logout get an automatic Slack DM reminder. No more dangling open records.
