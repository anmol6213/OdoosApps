COMMANDS

/login

Check-in. Optional grace minutes subtract from recorded time.

/logout

Check-out. Net working hours calculated and saved instantly.

/break

Start a break. Duration tracked. Breaks under threshold not deducted.

/resume

End break. Duration saved to attendance record in Odoo.

/login response

/break and /resume

/logout with net hours

FEATURES

Grace minutes

/login at 10:00 with 5-min grace records 9:55. Configurable per installation.

Break threshold

Breaks under the minimum (default 5 min) are saved but not deducted from net hours.

Net working hours

Net = Total − Counted breaks. Live in the Odoo attendance list and form view.

HMAC-SHA256 security

Every request verified with your Slack Signing Secret. Replay attacks blocked.

Per-employee timezone

Reads timezone from the HR employee record. No hardcoded values.

Missed logout alert

Daily cron sends a DM to anyone who checked in but never checked out.
